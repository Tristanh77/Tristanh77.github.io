About Me

It is my belief that technology should be able to be used by everyone. I take special appreciation for well designed apps that took hard work to create. I have spent years at Drexel University for Software Engineering as well as completed the 420 hour software engineering bootcamp at General Assembly. Dependability and passion is what I expect to carry me throughout my software engineering career.
